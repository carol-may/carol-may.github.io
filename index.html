ABOUT ELIXIR
Elixir is the socio-cultural fest of B.P.PODDAR INSTITUTE OF MANAGEMENT AND TECHNOLOGY, Kolkata. Over the years, Elixir has established a legacy of fellowship and pride which fosters cultural diversity and stimulates creativity and imagination. This spring we unveil a fest which shall witness days of absolute ecstasy, providing the budding artists a competing platform in diverse fields such as music, dance, theatre, photography, literature. So get ready to bask in the glory of a magnificence of an unprecedented scale and embrace all the fun and frolic because ladies and gentlemen, ELIXIR '19 will be unparalleled! Let 2019 spring bring to you the ’19 edition of Elixir which means 2019 will not just be another year, it will be THE year!
